Goldie Locks
and
The Three Bears

Once upon a time, there was a little girl named Goldilocks. One day, while walking in the forest she found a small cottage. She went inside and found three bowls of porridge cooling on the kitchen table. Goldilocks was hungry and decided to try some. The largest bowl was too hot. The medium bowl was too cold. The smallest bowl was just right for eating, so she gobbled up every last bite.

After such a gluttonous meal Goldilocks wanted to rest her feet. In the next room she found three chairs. The largest one was too splintery. The medium one was too stiff. The smallest one was just right for sitting, so she made herself comfortable. However, Goldilocks was too big and it broke into pieces.

Having dusted herself off, Goldilocks felt sleepy and went to the bedroom for a nap, where she found three beds. The largest one was as hard as bricks. The medium one was as soft as cotton. The smallest one was just right for sleeping, so she laid down and closed her eyes.

Soon, the three bears returned home. "Some one has been eating our porridge!" said papa bear. "Some one has been sitting on our chairs!" said mama bear. "Some one has been sleeping in our beds!" cried baby bear. And they are STILL here!"

Goldilocks woke up with a start and shrieked. She jumped up and ran outside, never looking back. The three bears never knew what became of Goldilocks or why she chose to trespass into their home. However, they always made sure to lock their front door before leaving home.

The End.
