About Wit Libs

A love for words and witticisms is the driving force behind this quaint and curious project. The various excerpts, synopses, and occasional paraphrasing that you may see on this website have been lovingly chosen to entertain and enthrall you. The visual layout of this website is designed to evoke the calming effect of a relaxed atmospere as you casually meander through the Libs. We hope that you will thoroughly enjoy these hopefully hilarius stories and visit our site again soon. Thank you and have a wonderfully word-filled day!
